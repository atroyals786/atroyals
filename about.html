Our Story

Born From a Passion
for Luxury

A&T Royal was founded with a single vision: to bring the finest Arabian attar tradition to modern fragrance lovers. What began as a small passion project in Rawalpindi grew into one of Pakistan's most trusted luxury attar brands.

Every bottle we craft is a testament to our commitment — sourcing only the purest raw materials, blending them with the skill of master perfumers, and delivering an experience that lasts long after the first application.

Today, we serve customers across Pakistan and beyond, united by their love for authentic, long-lasting, alcohol-free fragrances rooted in Arabian tradition.

Our Journey

A&T Royal Milestones

From a small dream in Rawalpindi to a nationally loved luxury attar brand.

2018 — The Beginning

Founded in Rawalpindi

A&T Royal was born from a deep love for Arabian fragrance tradition. Our first collection of 10 handcrafted attars launched to an overwhelmingly warm reception.

2020 — Expansion

Growing Our Collection

We expanded to over 100 unique fragrances, introducing exclusive Oud blends, premium musks, and our signature Eastern collection that quickly became bestsellers.

2022 — Going Digital

Reaching Pakistan Nationwide

Our online store launched, bringing A&T Royal fragrances to customers across all of Pakistan with fast delivery and dedicated customer support.

2024 — International Recognition

Global Fragrance Lovers

A&T Royal began shipping internationally, with loyal customers now in 30+ countries who trust our commitment to authentic, luxury Arabian attars.

2026 — Today

Continuing the Legacy

With 12,000+ happy customers and 500+ fragrances, we continue crafting premium attars that define luxury — and the journey is just beginning.
