Common Questions

We've compiled the most important answers to help you shop with confidence.

What makes A&T Royal attars different from regular perfumes?
Our attars are 100% alcohol-free, concentrated fragrance oils crafted in the traditional Arabian style. Unlike regular perfumes that contain 70–90% alcohol, our attars use premium natural and synthetic oils for longer lasting, richer scent profiles that evolve beautifully on your skin throughout the day.
Alcohol-Free Arabian Style Long Lasting
How long does the fragrance last on skin?
On average, our attars last 8 to 12+ hours depending on your skin type, weather conditions, and application method. Many customers report the scent lingering even longer on clothes and fabric. Drier skin may absorb fragrance faster — moisturizing before application significantly improves longevity.
8–12+ Hours Skin & Fabric
Are your attars safe for prayer and sensitive skin?
Yes, absolutely. All our attars are completely alcohol-free and formulated with skin-friendly, dermatologically considered ingredients. They are fully permissible to wear during prayers (Salah) and are suitable for most skin types including sensitive skin. If you have specific allergies, we recommend a patch test before full application.
Prayer-Safe Sensitive Skin No Alcohol
Are the bottles genuine and leak-proof?
Yes. We use premium glass bottles with high-quality atomizers and secure, airtight caps. Every bottle undergoes careful inspection before it is packed and shipped. Our packaging is designed to preserve fragrance integrity and withstand transit handling safely.
Premium Glass Leak-Proof Inspected
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently we ship across Pakistan only. We cover all major cities and towns through trusted courier partners. International shipping is being worked on and will be announced very soon — follow our WhatsApp channel for updates.
Pakistan-Wide International Coming Soon
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery typically takes 3–5 working days across Pakistan. Orders to major cities like Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, and Rawalpindi often arrive in 2–3 days. Once dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via WhatsApp.
3–5 Working Days Tracking Provided
Is free delivery available?
Yes! We offer free delivery on all orders above Rs 3,000. Orders below this threshold have a standard courier charge that varies by location. You can always confirm delivery costs before finalizing your order via WhatsApp.
Free Above Rs 3,000 All Pakistan
How should I apply attar for the best results?
Apply 2–3 dabs on your pulse points: wrists, neck, behind your ears, and chest. For longer-lasting projection, apply lightly on your clothes as well. For best results, apply immediately after a shower on clean, slightly moisturized skin — this allows the fragrance to bind deeply and last longer.
Pulse Points Post-Shower 2–3 Dabs
Can I layer different attars together?
Absolutely! Layering attars is a traditional Arabian practice called "tabaqqur." Apply a heavier, oudh-based attar as a base, then layer a lighter floral or citrus attar on top. Our fragrance experts on WhatsApp can guide you on the best combinations from our collection.
Layering Friendly Traditional Practice
How should I store my attar?
Store attars away from direct sunlight and heat — a cool, dry place is ideal, like a drawer or cabinet. Keep the cap tightly sealed when not in use. Properly stored, our attars retain their full potency and quality for 3–5 years or more.
Cool & Dark 3–5 Year Shelf Life
What is your return & exchange policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy provided the product is unused and in its original, sealed packaging. If you received a damaged or incorrect item, we'll arrange a replacement at no cost. Please contact us via WhatsApp before initiating any return so we can guide you through the process smoothly.
7-Day Window Unused & Sealed Free Exchange on Error
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) across Pakistan, as well as bank transfers and EasyPaisa / JazzCash for prepaid orders. Prepaid orders are given priority dispatch and may qualify for additional discounts. Contact us on WhatsApp to confirm available options.
Cash on Delivery EasyPaisa JazzCash Bank Transfer
